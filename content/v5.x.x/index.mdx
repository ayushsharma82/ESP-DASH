So as many would ask, what is ESP-DASH? In simple words - It's a <b>UI library</b> for ESP32, ESP8266, RP2040+W and RP2350+W microcontrollers that lets you create a fully capable on-device dashboard for your devices. It can be moulded in every way possible using cards, charts and many more ready-to-use features to suit your application.
